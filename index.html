Medication Reminder App

The Medication Reminder App was born out of a need to streamline the process of managing medication schedules. Recognizing a gap in the market for intuitive, user-friendly solutions, I developed this app using SwiftUI, EventKit, and UserNotifications. These tools were specifically chosen to offer real-time notifications and seamless synchronization with user calendars. One significant challenge was ensuring the app provided accurate reminders without overwhelming the user, and this was addressed by integrating the EventKit framework. The outcome was a user-friendly interface that improved medication adherence for users and was met with positive feedback.

Travel Planner Website

Seeking to simplify the complexities of travel planning, I led the creation of the Travel Planner Website. This project is a testament to my skills in integrating advanced APIs, such as OpenAI's GPT 3.5, into real-world applications. The challenge was to seamlessly amalgamate multiple data sources, including the Amadeus API for bookings, and presenting them in an engaging way. The solution involved a combination of Django for the backend and HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, Ajax, and JavaScript for an interactive frontend. One of the project's crowning achievements was its ability to generate custom itineraries through natural language processing, providing users with personalized travel experiences. This not only met but exceeded initial user expectations.

Jetpack Joyride Clone

Recreating the charm and engagement of a popular game like Jetpack Joyride was no easy task. My rendition, developed in Rust and Macroquad, showcases my ability to understand and emulate intricate game mechanics. A challenge faced was developing a system for generating dynamic obstacles, ensuring the game remained challenging yet engaging. By leveraging Rust's performance capabilities and integrating Macroquad for game development, I managed to create an experience reminiscent of the original. The distance tracking feature, a subtle addition, fostered a competitive spirit among players, leading to increased replayability and user engagement.
